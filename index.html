Gary's Teaching Philosophy

Gary believes that coding is not just about memorizing syntax; it's about understanding the logic behind it. In his classes, you won't find mind-numbing lectures but rather engaging sessions that foster curiosity and critical thinking. He encourages questions, discussions, and hands-on coding to ensure that his students truly grasp the essence of web development.
